DART398: Un.Bound in Perfect Print

Concordia University Fall 2020

168 Hours:A Polyvocal Book in The Era of Coronavirus

This is the final project for a bookmaking class taught by M. Wright at Concordia university titled "Un.bound in Perfect Print." It was in the Winter semester of the year 2020, and it was a strange year already. A global pandemic ravaged the globe. Justin Trudeau told us to stay home. This project was supposed to be something completely different, but the circumstances made me change it into a web-design project.
